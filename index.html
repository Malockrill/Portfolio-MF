LIL' ABOUT ME

Hello ! Welcome to my portfolio, i’m Malorie Fernandez, a developer with a passion for computer science. On this site you will find here some information about me and the projects i carry out.



Professional Goal

Since I was a little girl, I have always been fascinated by technology and computers. That's why I chose to train in computer science at a specialized school. My goal is to acquire solid skills in programming, cybersecurity and Web development to be able to create innovative and useful solutions.
In the long term, I would like to work in a technology company where I can contribute to ambitious and innovative projects. I also want to continue to learn and train throughout my career to stay at the forefront of new technologies.
On top of that, I'm motivated by the idea of inspiring other young girls to take an interest in careers in science and technology. I want to show that it is possible to succeed in this field and encourage more diversity in the IT professions.



Interests and Passions

I am passionate about Japanese culture, especially anime and manga, which teach me important values like perseverance and friendship through captivating stories and visually stunning universes. I also love music, which allows me to escape and feel a multitude of emotions through melodies and lyrics.
Movies are another passion of mine, because they transport me to imaginary worlds and make me live incredible adventures. Finally, I'm a big fan of video games, especially "Fortnite", "Hogwarts Legacy" and "Alice: Madness Returns", which allow me to develop my creativity, strategy and live immersive experiences.
All these passions teach me and teach me many things in life, showing me the importance of determination, imagination and the pleasure of discovering new things.
